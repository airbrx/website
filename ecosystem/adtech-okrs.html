Unlocking Business Value from Your Data



OKRs and KPIs

It is critical to understand the business goals to align the technoglogy to support those goals. In this case 'Adtech' operates in a highly competitive, fast-moving digital advertising ecosystem. To remain relevant and profitable, it needs to:

  • Leverage data as a core asset for innovation and operational scale.
  • Modernize its infrastructure to handle large volumes of real-time data.
  • Enable cross-functional teams with timely insights for smarter decision-making.
  • Drive measurable outcomes that align with revenue and product success.

These OKRs were designed to align teams around business-critical outcomes that transform how data powers every function—from engineering to analytics to go-to-market.



OKR 1: Build a Strategic Data Operations Foundation

Objective Establish a unified, scalable, and intelligent data platform to power operational efficiency, product innovation, and revenue growth.
KPI KR1.1 Complete migration of 80% of core data sources to a centralized Lakehouse.
KPI KR1.2 Reduce data engineering backlog by 50% through standardized pipelines.
KPI KR1.3 Launch Data Governance Council with quarterly reviews and published standards.
KPI KR1.4 Deliver 5 high-impact use cases powered by the new platform within 12 months.

OKR 2: Data & Platform Engineering

Objective Modernize data infrastructure to support real-time, high-volume adtech workloads and analytics.
KPI KR2.1 Consolidate 90% of pipelines under a single orchestration layer.
KPI KR2.2 Implement unified data catalog and lineage tools.
KPI KR2.3 Achieve >99% uptime and <5 min SLA for critical data pipelines.
KPI KR2.4 Deliver modular pipeline framework adopted by at least 3 teams.

OKR 3: Analytics & Data Science

Objective Empower teams with timely, trusted data for decision-making and model innovation.
KPI KR3.1 Deliver self-serve analytics layer with curated data marts.
KPI KR3.2 Automate data quality checks on 100% of production datasets.
KPI KR3.3 Reduce model experimentation and deployment time by 40%.
KPI KR3.4 Train 50+ team members via internal certification program.

OKR 4: Product & Business Enablement

Objective Use data to directly enable product value and revenue optimization.
KPI KR4.1 Launch real-time insights dashboard for 100% of top-tier clients.
KPI KR4.2 Operationalize LTV and churn models into 2+ core products.
KPI KR4.3 Improve campaign visibility through enhanced attribution instrumentation.
KPI KR4.4 Cut onboarding time for new publishers/advertisers by 30%.

OKR 5: Organizational Change & Data Culture

Objective Shift the company from siloed data thinking to a data product and platform mindset.
KPI KR5.1 Implement federated data product model in 3 business domains.
KPI KR5.2 Transition 75% of reporting off spreadsheets to governed BI tools.
KPI KR5.3 Run “Data Champions” program with reps from all functions.
KPI KR5.4 Conduct quarterly DataOps health checks with transparent updates.
