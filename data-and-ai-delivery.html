Agentic AI Implementation & Integration

AI is rapidly becoming a series of tools and technologies that work together. “Agentic AI” requires careful thought and execution to allow the models to make appropriate decisions including when to call in a human. Our team understands these danger zones and choke points and creates systems that enable your enterprise, not confuse or endanger the outcomes.

  • Enterprise AI Agent Development
    Our engineers build specialized agents for orchestration-aware use cases—e.g., auto-documenting DAGs, monitoring data SLA breaches, or surfacing insights via Slack/Teams integration. This generally takes 2-4 weeks per agent with full integration testing and documentation.
  • Agent + Pipeline Co-Execution
    We build pipelines that adapt and optimize themselves based on data characteristics and business context by integrating agents into Airflow DAGs or Databricks workflows to perform contextual augmentation, inline decision-making, or dynamic task generation based on data patterns and business rules.
  • Agent Guardrails & Human-in-the-Loop Integration
    Automation needs to be built correctly to ensure agent safety and intervention. Based on existing best practices and context of your enterprise, our team implements rule-based triggers, approval chains, and observability integrations with Astronomer Observe and Unity Catalog and creating clear audit trails and escalation procedures.
